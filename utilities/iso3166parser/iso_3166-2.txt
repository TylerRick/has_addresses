##
## ISO-3166-2 Standard for country subdivisions
##
## The following conventions are used in this file:
##
## If the standard lists a subdivision name in more than language
## and/or romanization system, only the first listed name is shown.
##
## Proper subdivision codes have the format 'XX-YYY' where XX is the
## ISO 3166-1 Alpha-2 country code and YYY is a 1-, 2- or 3-character
## code  for the subdivision.
##
## For some countries the standard also lists a second level of
## regional divisions (e.g. for BE, FR, GB, IT, etc.). The codes
## for these regional divisions are shown as 'XX YYYY', i.e. with a
## space character instead of the '-'.
##
## The subdivision names are UTF-8 encoded.
##
## The sorting order is the same that is used in the standard.
##
## Up-to-date as of newsletter, 2004-03-08, Newsletter 6.
